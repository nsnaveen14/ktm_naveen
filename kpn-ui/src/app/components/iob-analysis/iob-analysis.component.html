 help_outline Understanding Internal Order Blocks
arrow_upward Bullish IOB

The last bearish candle before a significant bullish move (Break of Structure upward). Indicates institutional buying interest. Look for LONG entries when price returns to this zone.

arrow_downward Bearish IOB

The last bullish candle before a significant bearish move (Break of Structure downward). Indicates institutional selling interest. Look for SHORT entries when price returns to this zone.

check_circle FVG (Fair Value Gap) - 6 Factor Validation

FVGs are validated using 6 factors: Unmitigated (zone not filled), Candle Reaction (proper close on retrace), S/R Confluence (overlaps with support/resistance), Priority by Position (lowest bullish / highest bearish = best), Gann Box Position (bullish in lower 50%, bearish in upper 50% of range), and BOS Before Gap (formed after Break of Structure). A score ≥ 50% = Valid FVG ✅.

swap_vert BOS (Break of Structure)

The swing high/low that was broken by the displacement move. This confirms the change in market structure and validates the IOB.
