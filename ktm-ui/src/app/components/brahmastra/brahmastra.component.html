help_outline How to Read These Charts
📈 Price & Supertrend Chart
  • Green candles = Price closed higher than open (bullish). Red candles = Price closed lower (bearish).
  • Supertrend line (green/red) acts as dynamic support/resistance. When the line is green (below price) → uptrend; red (above price) → downtrend.
  • Buy signal: When Supertrend flips from red to green (trend change). Sell signal: Green to red.
  • The farther price is from the Supertrend line, the stronger the trend. Price touching the line may indicate a reversal or retest.
📊 MACD Chart (12, 26, 9)
  • MACD Line (cyan) = Difference between 12-period and 26-period EMA. Signal Line (orange) = 9-period EMA of MACD.
  • Histogram bars: Green bars = MACD above Signal (bullish momentum); Red bars = MACD below Signal (bearish momentum).
  • Bullish crossover: MACD crosses above Signal line → potential buy. Bearish crossover: MACD crosses below → potential sell.
  • Growing histogram = increasing momentum. Shrinking histogram = momentum fading (possible reversal ahead).
  • Watch for divergence: Price making new highs but MACD making lower highs → bearish divergence (weakness).
💜 VWAP Chart
  • VWAP (purple line) = Volume Weighted Average Price — the "fair value" for the day.
  • Price above VWAP = Buyers in control (bullish bias). Price below VWAP = Sellers in control (bearish bias).
  • Dashed bands = ±1% standard deviation bands. Price outside bands may be overextended.
  • VWAP acts as a magnet — price tends to revert toward it. Use it as a dynamic support/resistance level.
  • Best used for intraday trading only — VWAP resets each session.
🎯 Triple Confirmation Strategy
  • Strong BUY: Supertrend = Green (Bullish) + MACD = Bullish Crossover + Price above VWAP.
  • Strong SELL: Supertrend = Red (Bearish) + MACD = Bearish Crossover + Price below VWAP.
  • 2 out of 3 indicators agreeing = moderate confidence. All 3 = high confidence (>70%).
  • Timeframe: Charts show 5-minute candles for the last 50 bars (~4 hours of market data).

help_outline How to Read the Signal Chart
🕯️ Reading Candlesticks
  • Each candle represents a 5-minute interval of price action. The X-axis shows IST market hours (9:15 AM – 3:30 PM).
  • Green candle: Open at bottom, Close at top (price went up). Red candle: Open at top, Close at bottom (price fell).
  • Thin lines (wicks) show the high/low range during that interval.
  • Tall candles with small wicks = strong conviction. Small body with long wicks = indecision.
🎯 Trade Level Annotations
  • ▲ BUY markers appear when all 3 indicators align bullish. ▼ SELL markers for bearish alignment.
  • Entry Price (blue line): The recommended entry level for the trade.
  • Stop Loss (red line): Exit if price crosses this level — limits your loss.
  • Target Levels (green lines): Profit-taking levels (Target 1 = conservative, Target 2 = extended).
  • Use toggles above to show/hide signals and trade levels for a cleaner view.
